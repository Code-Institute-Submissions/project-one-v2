Project Overview

The debut project at the Park is a development of three buildings, focused on providing homes for young professionals and couples. As well as studios and ensuites, two and three bed apartments are designed to provide equally sized bedrooms with ensuites for sharers.

The buildings are designed with enhanced sustainability credentials. The thermal performance exceeds building control requirements to lower heating costs for residents. The buildings provide heating and hot water from a combined heating and power plantroom. There are also solar panels on roofs.

Residents, as well as each apartment having their own balcony, have access to ample communal roof-top facilities for outdoor leisure. There is communal internal amenity at ground level, providing residents with access to reading rooms, cinema, working space and fitness rooms.
